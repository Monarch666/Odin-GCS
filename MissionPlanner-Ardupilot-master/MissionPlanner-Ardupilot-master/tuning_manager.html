About Basic Tuning
Too Low

Sluggish response, slow stick recovery, risk of frame instability.

Recommended

Balanced responsiveness, solid hovering accuracy, and wind rejection.

Too High

Aggressive stick tracking, high motor wear, and potential high-frequency oscillation.
